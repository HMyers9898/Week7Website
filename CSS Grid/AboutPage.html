When you first join the Scouts you are will be sent to a bootcamp. You will learn how to use your MP gear which is what helps us Scouts reach the weak spot in a titans neck. Our training last 2 years, it is extensive training to ensure you are ready to fight the titans and be as safe as possible while doing it. The photo to the right is of cadeets training, they are learning how you have to balance to use the MP gear, if you are unable to do this you will be disqualified. It is the most important part of our gear.
